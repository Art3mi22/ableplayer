Able Player for Vimeo
Example 1, Captions and Subtitles hosted on Vimeo

This page features an example of Able Player being used to play a Vimeo video. 4 The video featured is hosted in a Vimeo Pro account, required for hiding the controller and for being able to control playback rate with faster/slower buttons. Captions and subtitles in this demo are stored solely on Vimeo, therefore Able Player is unable to auto-generate an interactive transcript, and users have no control of the appearance of captions.

For more information about the limitations of hosting captions on Vimeo and YouTube, see the Able Player documentation.
